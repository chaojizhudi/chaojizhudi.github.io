Hi, this is Di Zhu. Welcome to my website!

I received the B.S. in Geographic Information Systems from Peking University and a dual B.S. in Economics from the National School of Development, Peking University. Currently, I'm pursuing the Ph.D. degree in GIScience at the Institute of Remote Sensing and GIS, Peking University.
I am a research assistant at the School of Earth and Space Science, Peking University. I have also worked as a visiting lecturer/researcher at SpaceTimeLab, Department of Civil, Environmental & Geomatic Engineering of University College London.
My research interests include Geospatial Modelling, Social Sensing, Spatiotemporal Data Mining and Applied Artificial Intelligence.
P.S. I'm a big fan of guitar, photography and NBA. :)
