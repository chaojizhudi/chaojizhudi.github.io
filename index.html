Hi, this is Di Zhu. Welcome to my website!

I received the B.S. in Geographic Information Systems from Peking University and a dual B.S. in Economics from the National School of Development, Peking University. Currently, I'm a last-year Ph.D. Candidate in GIScience at the Institute of Remote Sensing and GIS, Peking University. I will start my tenure-track position as an Assistant Professor at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities from August, 2020.
Since 2014, I have been a research assistant at the School of Earth and Space Science, Peking University. I have worked as a visiting lecturer/researcher at SpaceTimeLab, Department of Civil, Environmental & Geomatic Engineering of University College London between 2018 -2019.
My research interests include Geospatial Modelling, Applied Artificial Intelligence, Social Sensing, and Spatiotemporal Data Mining.
P.S. I'm a big fan of photography, music and NBA :)
