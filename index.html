Profile

Di Zhu was born in Mianyang, Sichuan Province, China. He received his B.S. in Geographic Information Systems (2014) from Peking University and B.S. in Economics (2014) from National School of Development, Peking University. He is currently a Ph.D. student in GIScience at Institute of Remote Sensing and GIS, Peking University.

His research interests include spatial heterogeneity, urban geography, deep learning and human mobility etc.
